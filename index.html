About

AI, robotics, and hardware-centric systems engineering.

I am an AI and Robotics student focused on building end-to-end robotic systems that tightly couple perception, decision-making, and actuation. My work spans computer vision, deep learning, and embedded control, with a strong emphasis on experimental validation.

Recently, I have been exploring vision-based fruit counting and quality assessment, depth reconstruction under motion blur, and terrain-aware planning from 3D point clouds. I enjoy working across the full stack: dataset design, model training, simulation, and deployment on microcontrollers and SBCs.
